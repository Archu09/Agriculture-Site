Disease Control:

For controlling diseases following chemicals are used. Preventive and control measures of the commonly occurring diseases in wheat

Name of Disease Preventive Measures Name of Chemicals
Smut
  1. Use of healthy seeds
  2. Roguing & burning of disease affected plants
  3. Use of resistant varieties
  4. Solar heat treatment
Seed dressing with Vitavax/Benlate/G 696
Rust
  1. Use of resistant varieties
  2. Timely sowing
 Zinc/Manganese
Bunts Use of resistant varieties Seed dressing with Agrosan GN/Vitavax
Powdery mildew Dusting with yellow sulphur, Sodium thiosulphate, Karathane/Miltox or spraying with Benlate Seed dressing with Agrosan GN/Vitavax
Molya
  1. Deep summer ploughing
  2. Adopt crop rotation
  3. Soil treatment
 Nemagon DECP 60% EC dripping with first irrigation water
